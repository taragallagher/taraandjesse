First off, everyone who is invited is also invited to bring a plus one. Beaver Island is a bit of a hike so everyone reserves the right to a travel buddy. Unless we have gotten in touch with you separately, there is room for you to stay on the island and no need to book housing in the area.

What is the schedule?

Friday: Games, big dinner, and orientation.
Saturday: Swim, sauna, hike, wallyball, yoga, marriage, happy hour, dinner, disco.
Sunday/Monday: Keep hanging out as long as people's schedules allow.

What do I pack?

Swimsuit, sneakers, portable charger if you have one, headlamp if you have one, towel, warm layers and rain gear (we'll keep an eye on the weather), clothes you don't mind getting muddy and sweaty in.

Wedding attire?

Any dress you feel comfortable in, or pants and a button down, but nothing too fancy. We'll be on grass so plan footwear accordingly.
